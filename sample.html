About the College

N.B.K.R INSTITUTE OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY was established in 2005 with the vision of providing world-class technical education. The institution offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Engineering, Technology, and Management. The campus provides modern classrooms, advanced laboratories, digital library, Wi-Fi connectivity, sports facilities, and experienced faculty members dedicated to academic excellence.
